# Acme Corp Travel Reimbursement Policy

## 1. Eligible Expense Categories
- Flights (economy class only)
- Hotels (max $150/night)
- Meals (max $60/day; $20 breakfast, $20 lunch, $20 dinner)
- Local transport (taxi, Uber, metro — max $50/day)
- Conference registration fees (pre-approval required)
- Internet/phone charges (max $20/day when traveling internationally)

## 2. Ineligible Expenses
- Business class or first class flights (unless flight > 8 hours, needs VP approval)
- Alcohol or personal entertainment
- Minibar charges
- Personal care items (haircuts, spa, gym)
- Fines or penalties (parking tickets, etc.)
- Family or personal travel companions

## 3. Per Diem Limits by City
- Tier 1 (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore): Hotel $120/night, Meals $50/day
- Tier 2 (Pune, Hyderabad, Chennai): Hotel $100/night, Meals $40/day
- Tier 3 (Other cities): Hotel $80/night, Meals $30/day
- International (US, UK, EU): Hotel $200/night, Meals $80/day

## 4. Approval Thresholds
- Up to $500: Auto-approved if policy compliant
- $501–$2000: Manager approval required
- $2001–$5000: Director approval required
- Above $5000: VP approval required

## 5. Receipt Requirements
- All expenses above $25 require a receipt
- Receipts must show: date, vendor name, amount, and purpose
- Missing receipts result in deduction of that expense
- Receipts must be submitted within 30 days of travel

## 6. Duplicate Claims
- Same vendor + same date + same amount = duplicate; will be rejected
- Partial duplicates flagged for manual review

## 7. Special Rules
- International travel requires pre-approval 5 days before departure
- Team meals: claimable once per day, max $30/person, needs attendee list
- Conference fees: must attach invitation or registration confirmation
